Autoboxing -
    1. The conversion of primitive types to the object of their corresponding wrapper class is known as Autoboxing.
    2. Example : conversion of int to Integer, long to Long, double to Double.

Unboxing - 
    1. Unboxing is a reverse process of Autoboxing. 
    2. Automatically converting an object of wrapper class to its corresponding primitive data type is known as Unboxing.
    3. Example : conversion of Integer to int, Long to long, Double to double
